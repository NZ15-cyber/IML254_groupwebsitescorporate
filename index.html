Welcome to RAHMAH Wedding Catering

Your wedding is a once-in-a-lifetime event — and it deserves to be joyful, delicious, and stress-free. At RAHMAH Wedding Catering, we specialize in turning your special day into a flavorful, affordable, and elegant celebration. Whether you’re planning a small garden majlis or a grand ballroom reception, our catering packages are designed to fit every budget and impress every guest.

We believe every couple in Malaysia should enjoy high-quality catering without worrying about costs. That’s why we offer customizable wedding menus starting from just RM12 per pax, with generous servings, friendly staff, and beautifully presented setups.

From traditional kampung favorites like Ayam Masak Merah and Daging Rendang to must-have dishes like Kambing Golek, we bring authentic taste and professional service to every wedding — big or small.

Explore our 15 pages to learn more about our menu, services, affordable packages, and past events. Let RAHMAH be your catering partner — affordable, trusted, and ready to serve with heart.
