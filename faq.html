Frequently Asked Questions

We know that planning a wedding comes with many questions. Below, we’ve answered the most common ones to help you make informed, confident decisions. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to contact us directly — we’re happy to assist!

❓ What is the price range for your catering packages?

Our packages start from RM12 per guest for Basic, RM18 for Standard, and RM25 for Premium. We also offer custom pricing based on your needs. No hidden fees — all prices include setup, staff, and equipment.

📅 How early should I book?

We recommend at least 1–3 months in advance, especially for weekends. We do accept last-minute bookings (minimum 2 weeks) depending on availability.

👨‍🍳 Can I customize the menu?

Yes! You can mix dishes, request vegetarian or allergy-friendly options, or include family recipes. We want your wedding meal to feel personal and special.

✅ Is your food 100% halal?

Absolutely. We only use halal-certified suppliers and follow strict hygiene and preparation practices so all guests can enjoy with confidence.

🍽️ Do you provide a food tasting session?

Yes! Complimentary tasting for 2 people with the Premium Package. For other packages, a small tasting fee applies — waived upon booking confirmation.

🎉 What’s included in the package?

All packages include food, setup, trays or dome sets, staff, drinks, basic skirting, and delivery. Premium packages include VIP tables, decor, and live cooking stations.

➕ Can I request extra food or a second round?

Yes! We offer top-up portions and refills depending on your package. Let us know in advance if you expect a larger crowd or want take-home options.

🔄 What happens in case of cancellation?

Deposits are non-refundable but can be transferred to a future date (subject to availability). Refunds depend on how early you cancel.

🗺️ Do you cover areas outside Kedah?

Yes, we cater to most parts of Kedah and nearby states depending on event scale. Transport charges may apply — just ask us!

✅ How do I confirm a booking?

Fill out our booking form or contact us directly. A 30% deposit secures your date, and the remaining balance is due 3–5 days before. We’ll provide a formal agreement and checklist after confirmation.

Still unsure? Reach out to our friendly team — we’re happy to help you plan a delicious, smooth, and memorable wedding experience!
