Meet the RAHMAH Team

Behind every delicious meal and smooth event is a passionate team. RAHMAH Wedding Catering is proudly led by four friends who share a vision — to make high-quality, affordable wedding catering accessible to everyone.

👩‍🍳 Zaty – Head of Operations

The logistics mastermind! Zaty handles bookings, event schedules, and customer coordination. Her attention to detail ensures every wedding runs without a hitch.

🍳 Yaya – Head Chef

The heart behind the kitchen. Yaya’s recipes blend tradition with technique, producing consistently delicious meals that guests always remember.

🎨 Aleya – Creative Director

From buffet styling to wedding color themes, Aleya turns ideas into elegant setups. She believes presentation should impress without extra cost.

🛠️ Zahir – Logistics & Setup

The early riser and hardworking hand behind every canopy, dome table, and serving line. Zahir ensures everything is set up on time and perfectly aligned.

Together, we are more than a business — we are a family that takes pride in every event we serve.
