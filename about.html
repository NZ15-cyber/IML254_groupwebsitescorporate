About RAHMAH Wedding Catering

“Crafting memories through flavor and heartfelt service.”

RAHMAH Wedding Catering is a family-driven business in Kedah, Malaysia — built on love, sincerity, and the joy of serving delicious meals. Founded by Zaty, Yaya, Aleya, and Zahir, our journey began with small family kenduris and blossomed into a name trusted across the north.

We’re committed to affordable luxury. We make sure every wedding feels high-end — without high prices. With generous portions, beautiful setups, and attentive staff, we’re here to make your day special.

Our packages cover everything — food, drinks, canopy setup, buffet lines, wait staff, and even live cooking stations. Every menu is halal. Every detail matters.

So, whether you’re planning a grand hall reception or a cozy backyard kenduri, know that with RAHMAH, you’re in good hands.

Let’s serve joy on a plate — together.
